Australia's Black Summer

The 2019-2020 bushfire season was one of the most catastrophic in recorded history. Unprecedented heat, prolonged drought, and extreme conditions converged to create a crisis that changed the nation forever.

18.6M Hectares Burned
3,000+ Homes Destroyed
34 Lives Lost
1B+ Animals Affected
